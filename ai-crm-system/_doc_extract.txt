AI-Powered Interaction Mining & CRM Automation Platform1. Business Requirement Document (BRD)1.1 ObjectiveTo design and implement an AI-powered system that automatically captures, transcribes, analyzes, and extracts structured insights from customer interactions across multiple channels (calls, emails, SMS, meetings), and seamlessly integrates them into CRM systems to enhance sales efficiency and decision-making.1.2 Business Problem- Manual CRM updates are time-consuming and error-prone.- Critical insights like budget, intent, and competitors are often missed.- Data is fragmented across multiple communication channels.- Lack of standardized data impacts forecasting and analytics.1.3 Proposed SolutionAn intelligent platform that:- Transcribes conversations into searchable text.- Uses AI/NLP to extract key sales entities.- Maps extracted data to CRM fields automatically.- Maintains a unified interaction history.- Provides actionable insights and revenue intelligence.1.4 Stakeholders- Sales Teams- CRM Administrators- Business Analysts- Revenue Operations- IT & Data Engineering Teams- Leadership1.5 Business Goals- Reduce manual CRM effort by 80%- Improve data accuracy and completeness- Enhance sales productivity- Enable better forecasting and decision-making1.6 Success Metrics (KPIs)- CRM auto-fill accuracy ≥ 90%- Reduction in manual data entry time- Increase in conversion rates- Improved forecasting accuracy2. Functional Requirement Document (FRD)2.1 Call Transcription ModuleFunctionality:- Convert audio/video calls into text- Speaker identification- Timestamp taggingInputs: Call recordingsOutputs: Structured transcripts2.2 AI Data Extraction ModuleExtracts:- Budget- Intent- Product- Competitors- IndustryOutputs structured JSON data for CRM mapping.2.3 Contextual Data MappingMaps extracted data to CRM entities such as Accounts, Contacts, and Opportunities.Includes entity resolution and duplicate detection.2.4 Automated Data Capture EngineCaptures interactions from:- Calls- Emails- SMS- MeetingsCreates a unified timeline of interactions.2.5 Revenue Insights ModuleGenerates insights such as deal progress, interaction impact, and revenue trends.2.6 Functional Flow1. Capture interaction2. Transcribe data3. Extract entities4. Map to CRM5. Auto-populate fields6. Store and visualize insights3. Data Requirement Document (DRD)3.1 Interaction DataFields:- Interaction ID- Type- Timestamp- Participants- Transcript3.2 Extracted Entities- Budget- Intent- Product- Competitor- Industry3.3 CRM MappingAccount: Company Name, IndustryContact: Name, EmailOpportunity: Deal Value, Stage, Intent3.4 Data FlowRaw Input → Transcription → AI Extraction → Mapping → CRM Storage3.5 Data Security- Encryption at rest and in transit- Role-based access control- Compliance (GDPR)- Audit logs3.6 Data Quality- Accuracy ≥ 90%- Deduplication- Validation rules- Error logging
